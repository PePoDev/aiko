# Aiko — Personal Finance Management Mobile App Project Design Document

## 1. Executive Summary

Aiko is a blue-themed personal finance management mobile app powered by a cute anime-style AI girl who helps users achieve wealth, success, and financial goals. The app combines daily money tracking, budgeting, goals, cash flow prediction, financial reporting, accounting, credit card management, asset allocation, tax organization, portfolio tracking, AI-based recommendations, and a full financial calculator suite.

Aiko should feel like a friendly financial companion: cute, supportive, smart, calm, and trustworthy. The app should make personal finance less stressful by turning complex financial data into simple dashboards, clear visual insights, and practical next steps.

### Core Promise
>
> Aiko helps you track money, build wealth, reduce waste, and reach your financial goals with friendly AI guidance.
>
### Product Positioning

Aiko is not only an expense tracker. Aiko is a **personal financial operating system** with a character-driven AI assistant experience.

### Main User Outcomes

- Understand where money goes every day.
- Stay on budget without feeling overwhelmed.
- Build savings and investment habits.
- Track net worth, assets, debts, credit cards, and portfolio performance.
- Predict cash flow, overspending risk, goal success, and future financial health.
- Receive personalized AI suggestions from Aiko.
- Use built-in calculators to plan loans, investments, retirement, taxes, and financial decisions.

## 2. Brand Concept and Identity

## 2.1 App Name

**Aiko** is the name of the app and the in-app AI assistant.
Aiko is designed as a cute anime-style AI girl who helps users organize their money, grow wealth, and stay motivated. She should feel like a supportive financial partner rather than a strict accountant.

### Brand Meaning

Aiko represents:

- Wealth growth
- Financial confidence
- Smart planning
- Emotional support
- Friendly guidance
- Success through consistency

### Brand Personality

Aiko should be:

- Cute
- Smart
- Trustworthy
- Calm
- Encouraging
- Optimistic
- Helpful
- Non-judgmental
- Motivational

### Brand Voice

Aiko should speak in a warm, clear, and supportive tone.
Instead of saying:

> You overspent this month.

Aiko should say:

> You are a little above your target this month. Let’s adjust together and get back on track.

Instead of saying:

> You failed your savings goal.

Aiko should say:

> You are still making progress. A small increase this week could help you reach your goal sooner.

- Build a complete personal finance management platform.

- Support both beginner and advanced finance users.

- Understand spending behavior.

- Save more money.
- Pay debt faster.

### Product Principles

2. **Aiko-guided:** AI should help users take the next best step.

4. **Privacy by design:** financial data must be protected.
5. **Actionable insights:** every insight should lead to a useful decision.
6. **Customizable experience:** users should control dashboard, budgets, goals, and reports.

## 9. Core Feature Design

## 9.1 Money Tracking

### Purpose

Allow users to record, import, classify, search, and review all money movement.

### Key Features

- Add income, expense, transfer, refund, investment, tax, and adjustment transactions.
- Manual entry with amount, date, account, category, note, tags, attachment, location, and merchant.
- Recurring transactions for salary, rent, subscriptions, insurance, utilities, and loan payments.
- Split transactions across multiple categories.
- Attach receipt images or documents.
- Search and filter by date, category, account, amount, merchant, tag, and transaction type.
- Duplicate transaction detection.
- Cash, bank, e-wallet, credit card, loan, investment, and asset account support.

### Transaction Types

- Income
- Expense
- Transfer
- Refund
- Investment Buy
- Investment Sell
- Dividend
- Interest
- Loan Payment
- Credit Card Payment
- Tax Payment
- Fee
- Adjustment

## 9.2 Effortless Transaction Entry

### Purpose

Make adding transactions fast, easy, and low-friction.

### Key Features

- Quick-add transaction button.
- Smart defaults based on past behavior.
- One-tap duplicate previous transaction.
- Voice input.
- Receipt scan input.

The main add flow should be amount-first:

## 24. Risks and Considerations

## 24.1 Product Risks

- Manual transaction entry can cause drop-off.
- Aiko’s anime style may not appeal to every finance user.
- AI recommendations may feel generic if data quality is low.

- Investment data requires accurate pricing and disclaimers.

## 24.2 Mitigation

- Start with a focused MVP.
- Use progressive disclosure for advanced features.
- Make dashboard customizable.
- Allow users to reduce or hide character visuals.
- Add import tools early.
- Use explainable AI suggestions.
- Keep Aiko friendly but professional.
- Use clear disclaimers for tax, investment, and AI suggestions.
