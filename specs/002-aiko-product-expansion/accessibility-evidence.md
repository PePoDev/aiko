# Accessibility Evidence

Validated expansion screens use native Flutter Material controls with accessible labels inherited from visible titles:

- Aiko character settings use segmented buttons and switches for visibility and reduced animation.
- Import/export/backup, bills, credit/debt, portfolio/assets, tax/accounting, learning, travel/devices, optimize, and monetization screens expose simple `ListTile` sections with readable text.
- Serious warning and tax copy avoid shame-based language and uses direct next-step wording.
- Navigation entries are reachable through GoRouter routes and Settings/Aiko entry points where required.

Future visual designs should preserve minimum tap targets, semantic chart summaries, and screen-reader alternatives for any custom chart or mascot artwork.
