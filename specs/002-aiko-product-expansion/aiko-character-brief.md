# Aiko Character Design Brief

Aiko is the product companion, not a decoration layer. She should appear where she helps users understand financial data, take a next step, or feel supported during onboarding, insights, reviews, and goal progress.

## Principles

- Data first, Aiko enhanced.
- Cute, calm, professional, and non-judgmental.
- Blue hair anchors the brand color system.
- Hide and reduced-visibility controls are always available.
- Serious financial warnings use practical, professional language.

## Approved Placements

- Onboarding introduction.
- Home greeting.
- Insight and recommendation cards.
- Monthly Aiko Review.
- Goal encouragement.
- Learning Hub guidance.
- Ask Aiko chat.

## Avoid

- Decorative placement without a financial purpose.
- Covering balances, charts, or warnings.
- Shame-based spending language.
- Overly playful language in tax, investment, debt, or security contexts.
