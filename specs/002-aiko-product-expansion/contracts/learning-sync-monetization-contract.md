# Contract: Learning, Sync, Travel, And Monetization

## Aiko Course Contract

Lessons must define topic, level, learning outcome, quiz or progress behavior, and why Aiko recommended the lesson.

## Device Sync Contract

Device sync must define device identity, last seen time, remote sign-out, sync status, conflict handling, and recovery behavior.

## Travel Mode Contract

Travel mode must define trip scope, local currency, home currency, exchange-rate source, fee handling, budget impact, and trip report behavior.

## Monetization Contract

Plan tiers must define included features, limits, upgrade moments, downgrade behavior, and data-portability guarantees.

Premium gating must not block:

- data export,
- account deletion,
- privacy controls,
- required disclaimers,
- critical warnings.
