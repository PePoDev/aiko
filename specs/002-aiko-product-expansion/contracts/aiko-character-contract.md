# Contract: Aiko Character System

## Purpose

Define how Aiko appears and speaks across expansion modules without distracting from financial data.

## Placement Contract

Each Aiko placement must define:

- purpose,
- screen context,
- visual prominence,
- expression or state,
- reduced/hidden behavior,
- accessibility label or alternative,
- serious-warning handling.

## Tone Contract

Aiko copy must be:

- warm,
- clear,
- supportive,
- non-judgmental,
- accurate about risk,
- concise in alerts,
- educational in learning contexts.

## Prohibited Behavior

Aiko must not:

- cover important numbers or charts,
- soften critical risks until they become unclear,
- imply guaranteed financial outcomes,
- appear decoratively where no guidance is needed.
