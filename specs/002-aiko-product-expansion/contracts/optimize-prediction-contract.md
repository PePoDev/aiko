# Contract: Aiko Optimize And Prediction

## Purpose

Define safe, explainable optimization and prediction outputs for advanced Aiko guidance.

## Suggestion Contract

Each suggestion must include:

- title,
- recommendation,
- source data summary,
- expected impact,
- confidence or uncertainty,
- assumptions,
- user action,
- dismiss/tune feedback,
- disclaimer type.

## Prediction Contract

Predictions must identify:

- forecast period,
- input data,
- assumptions,
- confidence range,
- scenario class,
- stale or missing data,
- user-facing limitation.

## Safety Contract

Aiko Optimize must not present estimates as guarantees or certified financial, tax, legal, or investment advice.
